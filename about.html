About Fun Farm Friends

Do you want a fun and interactive way to get your students to keep the noise level at a minimum? With the help of Fun Farm Friends you can give your students a visual representation of the noise level your classroom, thus creating a better learning enviroment. You have the freedom to control how sensitive you want the microphone to be and how many seconds at that noise level for a fun animal to appear. With a live score keeper on the screen your kids will get to set goals to reach through out the day. At the end of each day their score final score with appear and encourage kids to beat previous scores of the days before. Take control of your classroom and learning enviroment today!
